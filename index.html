Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a GPU for licensing?

Any CUDA, ROCm, or MPS device where NesionEngine is actively evicting Cache tokens during inference in a production environment. Developer machines, test clusters, and idle instances do not count toward your license limit.

Can I use Nesion for free?

Yes. Research, personal projects, and production deployments utilizing fewer than 2 GPUs are always free under the MIT license terms provided in our repo.

What happens if I don't get a license?

For clusters ≥2 GPUs, Nesion will display a console warning but continue to function seamlessly for a 30-day grace period. Continued commercial use without a valid license after this period violates the commercial terms.

Can I try before buying?

Yes. All Enterprise licenses include a 12-day free trial period. No credit card is required to generate the key and start testing your specific model workloads.
